About
I started as a math teacher in public school, moved into curriculum design at Khan Academy, and eventually built my own courses on AI and statistics. I believe the best explanation is the one that makes the reader feel smart — not the one that proves the writer is. Right now I'm writing a book, running a newsletter, and occasionally consulting for EdTech companies who want their content to actually work.
